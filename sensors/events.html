RULES

1. The participants are required to capture any of the day to day processes and provide a description for the captured image in terms of the technical phenomenon they are aware of.

2. The participants can use one or more photographs of the same subject shot in different angles to illustrate the phenomena better.

3. You are free to use any camera(Professional/mobile) to click pictures.

4. All the pictures must have accurate captions and technical description ,written in English only.

5. Using Photoshop to bring in any superficial effects is strictly not allowed.

6. Entries should send the following info along with the picture:

  • -> Name
  • -> College Name and Location
  • -> Contact Number
  • -> Email
  • -> Detailed description of the subject matter
7. The last date for submitting the entries is 5th January 2018

8. Submissions will not be accepted once the deadline lapses.
