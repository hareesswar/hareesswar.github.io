Have you ever pondered how the world would seem if you could read people’s minds? Don’t worry, it is exactly what we are going to show you during the entire span of 3 hours; using just basic electronics and software concepts. We will be teaching the basic working of an Arduino microcontroller during the workshop after which we would introduce you to the EEG sensor, which is used for retrieving brain signals. Also, we would be dealing with signal conditioning concepts that would be required to retrieve the brain signals. We would compute the frequency of the signals and would classify the signals into Alpha, Beta, Gamma etc. which indicate various states of the brain such as sleeping, alert state etc., and can be used to assess your sleep quality.
The content that would be covered includes the following: (slightly difficult in 3 hours though) Basics of Arduino MCU Brief description of EEG sensor. Concept of amplifiers and noise removal (Analog Electronics) Plotting signals and computing frequency of a signal.
